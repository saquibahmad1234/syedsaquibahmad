About

I’ve always been interested in understanding money beyond numbers — how it shapes choices, anxiety, freedom, and long-term thinking. Over time, this curiosity naturally extended into taxation, investing, and personal finance.

I don’t believe financial decisions should feel rushed, opaque, or overwhelming. Most mistakes with money don’t happen because people lack intelligence — they happen because clarity is missing.

This website serves two purposes. One, it’s a place where I write — to think clearly, document ideas, and share what I’m learning. Two, it’s where I work with people who want thoughtful, grounded guidance around taxes, investments, and financial planning.

If you’re here to read, I hope something here helps you see money a little more clearly. If you’re here to work together, I believe good outcomes start with good conversations.
