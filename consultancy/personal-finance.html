Personal Finance

I think about personal finance less as optimisation and more as structure. Most financial stress doesn’t come from low income or bad investments — it comes from unclear systems and rushed decisions.

This page brings together how I think about personal finance, the kinds of problems I spend time on, and writing that explores money decisions with patience and long-term perspective.

What I focus on

  • Building clarity around cash flows and obligations
  • Creating simple, sustainable financial structures
  • Making decisions that age well over time
  • Avoiding noise-driven financial choices
