Taxation

I think about taxation less as a compliance exercise and more as a decision-making problem. Most tax mistakes don’t come from ignorance — they come from acting before understanding consequences.

This page brings together how I approach taxation, the kind of questions I spend time on, and writing that explores tax-related decisions with clarity and restraint.

What I focus on

  • Understanding tax implications before decisions are made
  • Planning with context, not shortcuts
  • Interpreting rules calmly instead of reacting to them
  • Avoiding urgency-driven tax decisions
