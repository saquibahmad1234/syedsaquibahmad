This book is about the value of focused, uninterrupted work in a world
designed to fragment attention.

## What stood out

- The emphasis on systems, not motivation
- The cost of shallow work
- The practical rituals suggested

## Who should read this

This is especially useful if your work requires thinking, writing, or
problem-solving without constant interruptions.
